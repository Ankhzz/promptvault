---

| Time | Visual | Voiceover |
|---|---|---|
| 0-3s | Landing page: hero section with 3D vault rotating, headline "Encrypted AI Prompt Vaults" | "This is PromptVault — threshold-encrypted vaults for your AI prompts, built on Story Protocol." |

| 27-30s | Theme toggle to light mode → zoom out to full landing page | "Built entirely on Story Protocol for the CDR Hackathon. Dark mode, light mode — your choice." |

## Notes for Recording

- Use dark mode for the main demo (it looks better on video)
- Screen resolution: 1920×1080
- Pre-connect your wallet before recording to avoid delays
- Browser: Chrome with clean bookmarks bar
- Cursor: use an enlarged cursor (Chrome DevTools → Rendering → Emulate a focused page)
- Audio: clear, steady pace. No background music to keep it professional.
- Export as MP4, upload unlisted to YouTube or Loom, link in submission
