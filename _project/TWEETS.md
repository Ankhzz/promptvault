## Tweet 2 — May 19-20 · "Tech Peek"

> Deep dive into PromptVault's vault types 🧵
>
> 🟦 **Licensed** — Register an IP Asset on Story, mint license tokens. Only token holders can decrypt your prompts.
>
> ⚫ **Private** — Owner-only. Your wallet, your prompts. Maximum privacy, no exceptions.
>
> 🟡 **Time-Locked** — Smart contract enforces an unlock timestamp. Anyone can decrypt after the deadline.
>
> All three use CDR threshold encryption — no single validator holds the full key.
