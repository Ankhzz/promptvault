**Tweet 2 — The Problem**

Prompt engineers spend hours crafting the perfect prompt.

But there's no way to protect that IP without trusting a centralized server — which defeats the purpose of web3.

CDR changes this.

**Tweet 3 — How It Works**

1️⃣ Create a vault → choose your access model (license token, wallet-only, or timelock)

2️⃣ Your prompt is encrypted client-side with a random 256-bit key

3️⃣ The key is threshold-encrypted to the CDR validator network

4️⃣ Only authorized wallets can request decryption — validators threshold-decrypt

No backdoors. No single point of failure.

**Tweet 4 — Vault Types**

Three ways to gate your content:

🔵 Licensed Vaults — IP registration + license token minting on Story. Sell access to your prompts.

⚫ Private Vaults — Owner-only. Your wallet, your prompts, no exceptions.

🟡 Time-Locked Vaults — Smart contract enforces an unlock timestamp. Perfect for scheduled releases.
